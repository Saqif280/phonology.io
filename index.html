Why Phonology

Humans are historically bad at remembering long strings of meaningless numbers. However, some numbers are worth remembering. That's why Phonology.io was developed. Instead of remembering long strings of integers, remember a unique phrase that we generate for the contacts worth always knowing. When you need the number again, just come back here and we'll translate it for you.
